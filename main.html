Paris

Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most popular travel destinations in the world. Known for its romantic ambiance, rich history, world-class museums, and stunning architecture, it offers an unforgettable holiday experience.

  1. Eiffel Towerb – the most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a vibrant avenue perfect for shopping and sightseeing.
  5. Seine River Cruise – enjoy the beauty of Paris from the water.
  • Croissants – buttery, flaky pastries enjoyed at breakfast.
  • Escargot – a French delicacy made from snails.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory flavors.
  • Ratatouille – a traditional vegetable dish full of flavor.
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate French cookies.
